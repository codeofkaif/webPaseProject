masti day at nani's house

"Mere pyare bhatiji ka Nani ke ghar ka pahla din ek yaadgaar tha. Sirf 3 mahine ka chhota sa gudiya jaisa wo baccha, apni maa ki god se utar kar jab Nani ke kora me gaya, to jaise uske chhote se chehre par ek alag hi sukoon chha gaya.
Uski chhoti-chhoti ungliyan Nani ki saadi ke kone ko pakadne ki koshish kar rahi thi, aur uski gol-matol aankhen duniya ko naye nazariye se dekh rahi thi. Nani usse apne dil se lagaye, jaise wo uska apna ek hissa ho. Unhone jaise uske chhote se gale me apne pyaar ka haar pehna diya ho.
Aur wo chhoti si muskaan! Usne sabka dil jeet liya. Ghar bhar ke log uske aas-paas ghoom rahe the, aur wo apne chhote haathon se hawa me kuch pakadne ki koshish kar raha tha. Nani ke kora me woh masoomiyat aur khushi dekh kar aisa laga jaise zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat pal samne ho.
Mera ye pyara bhanja to sach me ghar ka chhota sa chaand hai, jo har pal roshan karta rahta hai."

First day in this world

"27 November 2023, Monday, subah ke lagbhag 3 baje, wo pal aaya jo humari zindagi ka sabse bada imtihaan aur sabse badi khushi ban gaya. Mere dil ke ek tukde, meri pyari beti, is duniya mein aayi. Lekin us raat ki kahani sirf khushi ki nahi, balki ek dar aur duaon se bhare safar ki bhi thi.
Doctor ne jab kaha ki halat critical hai, to meri saans rukh si gayi thi. Ek ajeeb si bechaini thi, jaise sab kuch mere haath se nikal raha ho. Meri khala, mojammil ki ammi, aur meri ammi sab mere saath the. Har koi bas ek hi dua kar raha tha ki meri beti theek se is duniya mein aaye. Meri ammi to itni pareshaan thi ki unhone pani tak nahi piya, aur sab log bas dil thame baithe the.
Aur phir wo pal aaya, jab doctor ne kaha ki beti sahi-salamat paida ho gayi hai. Us waqt jo sukoon mila, uska koi shabdon mein bayan nahi. Aankhon se aansoon beh rahe the, magar is baar khushi ke the. Sabne milkar Allah ka shukar ada kiya, kyunki unhone humari dua kabul kar li.
Meri pyari beti, tumhare paida hone ke pal ne hume zindagi ka ek sabse bada tohfa diya. Tum sirf ek beti nahi, balki Allah ki rehmat ho jo humari zindagi ko roshan kar gayi. Allah se bas yahi dua hai ki tumhe hamesha khush aur sehatmand rakhe. Tumhare wajah se wo raat hamesha ke liye yaadgaar ban gayi."
